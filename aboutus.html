About EcoGuard

EcoGuard is a pioneering initiative dedicated to empowering communities with knowledge about pesticides used in their neighborhoods. Our mission is to promote environmental awareness and public health by providing accessible, comprehensive information on pesticides and their impacts.

Founded in 2023 by a team of environmental scientists, data analysts, and community advocates, EcoGuard aims to bridge the gap between scientific research and public understanding of pesticide use and its consequences.

Our Mission

To provide transparent, accurate, and user-friendly information about pesticides, enabling individuals and communities to make informed decisions about their environment and health.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to clear, scientific information about the chemicals in their environment, leading to healthier ecosystems and informed citizen engagement in environmental policies.
